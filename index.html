Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

Transparency is our priority. We believe in clear communication about how our extension handles your data. This document explains our privacy practices in simple terms.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser, ensuring your information remains private and secure.

Information Stored Locally

The extension stores the following information locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • Your preferred job search locations
  • Refresh interval settings
  • Work hour preferences
  • Extension activity logs

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

We only interact with hiring portals to enhance your job search experience. No external servers, analytics services, or tracking technologies are used.

Permissions Explanation

Our extension requires minimal permissions to function properly:

storage Save your preferences locally in your browser
activeTab Interact with hiring pages to enhance functionality

We request only the permissions necessary for core functionality and nothing more.
